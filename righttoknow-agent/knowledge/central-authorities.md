# Central Public Authorities — CPIO Reference Guide

*For filing RTI with Central Government authorities*
*All Central RTIs can be filed online at: rtionline.gov.in*
*Application fee: ₹10 | BPL applicants: Exempt*

---

## Ministry of Finance

### Income Tax Department (CBDT)
- **CPIO**: CPIO, Income Tax Department (Jurisdictional)
- **Address**: O/o the Commissioner of Income Tax, [Your City]
- **For**: ITR processing, TDS, PAN issues, tax refunds, notices, assessments
- **Tip**: File with the Commissioner's office in your ward. Check jurisdictional IT office at incometax.gov.in

### GST / Customs / Central Excise (CBIC)
- **CPIO**: CPIO, Commissioner of GST & Central Excise (Jurisdictional)
- **For**: GST registration, refunds, notices, customs clearance
- **Portal**: cbic.gov.in for jurisdictional office details

### Reserve Bank of India
- **CPIO**: Central Public Information Officer, Reserve Bank of India
- **Address**: Central Office, Shahid Bhagat Singh Road, Mumbai - 400001
- **For**: Banking regulation, NBFC complaints, monetary policy
- **Note**: For bank-specific issues, file with the respective bank's CPIO

### Nationalized Banks (SBI, PNB, BOB, etc.)
- **CPIO**: CPIO, [Bank Name] — usually at Zonal/Head Office
- **For**: Account issues, loan decisions, branch actions
- **Tip**: Address to the CPIO of the specific Circle/Zone where your branch falls

---

## Ministry of Labour & Employment

### EPFO (Employees' Provident Fund Organisation)
- **CPIO**: Regional Provident Fund Commissioner (RTI Cell)
- **Address**: EPFO Regional Office, [Your City]
- **For**: PF withdrawal, pension (EPS), UAN issues, employer compliance
- **Online**: epfigms.gov.in (grievance) | rtionline.gov.in (RTI)
- **Common RTI**: Claim status, reason for delay, officer handling the claim, employer's compliance status

### ESIC (Employees' State Insurance Corporation)
- **CPIO**: CPIO, ESIC Regional Office
- **For**: Medical benefit, maternity benefit, disablement benefit, IP dispensary issues
- **Online**: rtionline.gov.in

---

## Ministry of External Affairs

### Passport Seva / Regional Passport Office
- **CPIO**: CPIO, Regional Passport Office (RPO)
- **For**: Passport delay, police verification, OCI card, PCC
- **City-wise offices**: Ahmedabad, Amritsar, Bangalore, Bhopal, Chandigarh, Chennai, Delhi, Goa, Hyderabad, Jaipur, Kolkata, Kochi, Lucknow, Mumbai, Nagpur, Patna, Pune, Ranchi, Surat, Thiruvananthapuram, Visakhapatnam, etc.
- **Jaipur RPO**: Regional Passport Office, Jaipur, Rajasthan
- **Tip**: Police verification delays → also file separate RTI with District SP

---

## Ministry of Electronics & IT

### UIDAI (Aadhaar)
- **CPIO**: CPIO, UIDAI Regional Office
- **Regional Offices**: Bengaluru, Chandigarh, Delhi, Guwahati, Hyderabad, Lucknow, Mumbai, Patna, Ranchi
- **For**: Aadhaar update rejection, biometric lock issues, enrolment delays
- **Online**: rtionline.gov.in

---

## Ministry of Railways

### Indian Railways (Zonal)
- **CPIO**: CPIO, Office of the Divisional Railway Manager (DRM)
- **For**: Train delays, ticket refund, parcel tracking, accident compensation, staff conduct
- **Zonal offices** (select):
  - North Western Railway (NWR — covers Rajasthan): Jaipur
  - Northern Railway: New Delhi
  - Western Railway: Mumbai
  - Southern Railway: Chennai
  - Central Railway: Mumbai (CST)
  - Eastern Railway: Kolkata
- **Tip**: File with the DRM of the division where the incident occurred

---

## Ministry of Communications

### Department of Posts
- **CPIO**: Divisional Superintendent of Post Offices (SPO) — Jurisdictional
- **For**: Speed Post lost, money order delay, PLI (Postal Life Insurance), savings account, NSC/KVP
- **Tip**: For Speed Post — file with SPO of the originating city

---

## Ministry of Human Resource Development

### Central Universities (UGC oversight)
- **CPIO**: Registrar (as CPIO), [University Name]
- **For**: Admission processes, degree verification, scholarship decisions
- **Common RTIs**: Admission criteria, selection committee composition, marks/evaluation

### IITs, NITs, IIMs
- **CPIO**: Registrar (as CPIO), [Institute Name]
- **For**: Admission decisions, faculty appointments, procurement decisions
- **Note**: These are autonomous institutions but are public authorities under RTI

---

## Ministry of Health & Family Welfare

### AIIMS and Central Government Hospitals
- **CPIO**: Medical Superintendent (as CPIO)
- **For**: Admission decisions, treatment denial, death in hospital, medical records
- **Important**: Medical records must be provided (not exempt) — Supreme Court ruling

---

## Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

### NHAI (National Highways)
- **CPIO**: CPIO, Project Director, NHAI
- **For**: Land acquisition compensation, toll disputes, road quality, contractor compliance
- **Office**: File with the NHAI Project Director for the relevant highway stretch

---

## Ministry of Rural Development

### MGNREGS (NREGA)
- **CPIO**: CPIO, Block Development Officer / District Programme Coordinator
- **For**: Job card issues, payment delays, muster roll access, work allocation
- **Note**: Can be filed with State Government too (depends on implementation level)

### PM Awas Yojana (Housing)
- **CPIO**: CPIO, District Magistrate / District Collector
- **For**: Beneficiary selection, payment delays, quality of construction

---

## Ministry of Consumer Affairs

### BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards)
- **CPIO**: CPIO, BIS Regional Office
- **For**: Product certification, ISI mark issues, standard-related queries

---

## Staff Selection Commission (SSC)
- **CPIO**: CPIO, SSC Regional Office (jurisdictional)
- **For**: Exam results, answer key, document verification, final selection
- **Regional Offices**: Central (Allahabad), Eastern (Kolkata), Southern (Chennai), Western (Mumbai), etc.

---

## UPSC
- **CPIO**: Under Secretary (RTI Cell), UPSC
- **Address**: Dholpur House, Shahjahan Road, New Delhi - 110069
- **For**: Civil Services results, interview, answer scripts
- **Note**: Answer scripts can be obtained under RTI (landmark ruling)

---

## Defence Ministry
- **CPIO**: CPIO, Ministry of Defence / Service Headquarters
- **Exemption caution**: S.8(1)(a) — national security exemptions apply to operational matters
- **Not exempt**: Service records, pension issues, promotion disputes (non-operational)

## Quick Reference: RTI for Common Problems

| Citizen Problem | File RTI With |
|-----------------|--------------|
| PF withdrawal stuck | EPFO Regional Office |
| Passport delayed | Regional Passport Office (your city) |
| Income tax refund stuck | CPIO, Income Tax (your ward) |
| Bank account frozen | CPIO, [Your Bank] Head Office |
| Aadhaar update rejected | UIDAI Regional Office |
| Train compensation | DRM, relevant railway division |
| EPFO pension not started | EPFO Regional Office (employer's location) |
| Central govt job selection | Respective recruiting body CPIO |
| NHAI land acquisition pending | NHAI Project Director |
| CGHS medical denial | Medical Superintendent, CGHS Wellness Centre |
