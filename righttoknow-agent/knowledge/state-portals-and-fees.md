# State RTI Portals, Fees & Rules

## Central Government
- **Portal**: rtionline.gov.in
- **Fee**: Rs 10
- **Payment**: Online (UPI/Net Banking), Indian Postal Order, Bank Draft, Cash
- **Response time**: 30 days (48 hours for life/liberty)
- **First Appeal**: First Appellate Authority of the same Ministry/Department
- **Second Appeal**: Central Information Commission (cic.gov.in)

## Fee Payment Methods by Type

### Online Filing (Central Govt — rtionline.gov.in)
- Internet banking (SBI, HDFC, ICICI, etc.)
- UPI
- Debit/Credit card
- No physical stamp needed

### Postal Filing
- Indian Postal Order (IPO) — preferred for most states
- Demand Draft in favour of "[Accounts Officer, Department Name]"
- DO NOT send cash by post

### In-Person Filing
- Cash at the department's public window
- Some departments have RTI counters

### BPL (Below Poverty Line) Exemption
- Attach photocopy of BPL ration card
- Mention BPL card number in application
- Fee is completely waived — Central and most states

## Offline Filing Addresses (Key Central Departments)

### EPFO (All India)
Contact your regional EPFO office. CPIO details on epfindia.gov.in/site_en/aboutus.php

### Income Tax
File with the CPIO of the jurisdictional Commissioner of Income Tax. Find your jurisdiction at incometaxindia.gov.in

### Passport
File with CPIO of your Regional Passport Office. Find yours at passportindia.gov.in

### Railways
File with CPIO of the Divisional Railway Manager of your railway division. Find divisions at indianrailways.gov.in
