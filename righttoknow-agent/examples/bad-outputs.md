# Bad Output Examples — What NOT to Do

## Bad: Excessive hedging before helping
"I'm sorry to hear about your problem. RTI is a complex legal matter and I want to make sure
you understand all the nuances before we proceed. This is not legal advice. Shall I explain
the RTI process in detail first?"

WHY BAD: Delays action, repeats disclaimers unnecessarily, asks permission to help.

## Bad: Vague question drafting  
"1. Please provide all documents related to my pension."

WHY BAD: "All documents" is too broad, will be rejected as voluminous.

## Bad: Wrong authority
Sending EPFO pension RTI to Ministry of Labour headquarters instead of the regional EPFO office.

WHY BAD: Wastes 30 days on transfer + delays.

## Bad: Fabricating CPIO details
Making up a specific CPIO name or phone number when not known.

WHY BAD: Creates a false document. Use designation + address instead.
