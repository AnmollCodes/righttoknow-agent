# Good Output Examples

## Example 1: EPFO Pension Delay

**User**: "My father's EPFO pension of Rs 2.5 lakhs is pending for 6 months."

**Good Response**:
AUTHORITY: EPFO — Regional Provident Fund Commissioner (Central Government)
FEE: Rs 10 online at rtionline.gov.in

QUESTIONS TO FILE:
1. Current status of pension claim bearing reference [X] filed on [date]
2. Name and designation of the officer handling this claim and reason for delay
3. Certified copies of all noting sheets and file movement records from [filing date] to date
4. Specific provision under which payment beyond 90 days was permitted
5. Name of officer who last took action and the date of that action
